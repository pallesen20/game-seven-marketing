Questions

Good to know.

Answers to the most common questions about our audit services.

Are these automated reports or manual audits?

Manual. Every audit is conducted by a senior consultant who reviews your site directly. We use tools to assist the analysis, but findings, prioritisation, and recommendations are written by a human - not auto-generated and emailed.

Which audit should I start with?

If you've never had a professional SEO review, the Full Site Audit gives you the most complete picture. If you have a specific concern - a slow site, weak content, missing AI visibility - the specialist audits are faster and more focused. If budget is the constraint, the Single Page Audit on your highest-traffic page is a strong starting point.

What do I need to provide?

Just your website URL. We don't need CMS admin access, Google Analytics, or Search Console for most audits. If you'd like to share access for deeper analysis, you can note that after checkout - but it's not required.

What's the difference between the Full Site Audit and the specialist audits?

The Full Site Audit covers all six dimensions - technical, content, schema, AI search, on-page, and site architecture - across your entire website. The specialist audits go deep on one dimension across your key pages. Many clients run a specialist audit first, then upgrade to the Full Site Audit once they've seen the value.

What is AI search visibility and why does it matter?

AI search refers to how your brand appears in results from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. An increasing share of buying-intent queries are being answered by AI systems directly - without the user clicking through to your site. If your brand isn't being mentioned or recommended, you're invisible to that traffic.

Can I upgrade to an ongoing engagement after an audit?

Yes. Many clients use an audit as a starting point before commissioning ongoing SEO, AEO, or CRO work. Just reply to the report email and we'll set up a call.
