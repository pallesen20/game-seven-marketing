Everything you need to know before we talk.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Technical SEO fixes can show ranking improvements within weeks. Content and authority-building typically compounds over 3–6 months, after which it accelerates significantly. Most clients see meaningful organic traffic increases within 90 days and substantial revenue impact by month 4–6. Unlike paid ads, the results don't stop when you stop paying — they keep growing.

What exactly is AI Optimization (AEO)?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) means structuring your content, brand authority, and data so that AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews recommend your products when users ask questions in your category. It's the fastest-growing new acquisition channel in e-commerce, and the brands that establish AI visibility now will compound that advantage for years.

Do you work with brands at any stage?

For CRO specifically, we recommend at least 10,000 monthly sessions to run statistically valid A/B tests. For SEO and AEO, we work with brands at any stage — building the right technical foundation early is far easier than fixing a broken structure later. Book a call and we'll tell you exactly what's right for where you are now.

What's your performance guarantee?

Within 6 months, we guarantee a measurable improvement in your revenue per visitor from organic channels. If we haven't hit that goal, we keep working at no additional charge until we do. We put our own time on the line because we're confident enough in our process to back it up.

You've built and sold e-commerce brands — how does that help me?

It means we understand the pressure you're under. We know what a Google algorithm update feels like when it's your own revenue on the line. We've made expensive Shopify mistakes — so we won't make them on your store. We think about your business like owners, not service providers. That's a different kind of engagement.

Do you work on platforms other than Shopify?

Shopify and Shopify Plus are our primary platforms and where we do our deepest, most effective work. We can provide SEO and CRO consulting for WooCommerce and BigCommerce stores, but if you're on Shopify, you'll get the full benefit of our platform-specific expertise — which goes considerably deeper than what any generalist agency can offer.
